Title

weSTEM at NYU Abu Dhabi supports women and non-binary individuals interested in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics by building a community that shares knowledge, encourages persistence, and inspires each other to grow together. Our mission is to increase the participation and success of women and non-binary individuals interested in the STEM fields at our university and in the region.
To achieve this mission and build an empowering community, we focus on four areas:

1

Creating an environment that encourages a culture of allyship and support for one another.

2

Demonstrating the value of diversity, equity, and inclusion in the STEM fields and creating cultural change across NYUAD and beyond.

3

Supporting students in their academic and professional careers by providing networking and leadership development opportunities, mentoring, and resources.

4

Inspiring young students in the region to learn more about STEM fields by hosting workshops and conferences.
